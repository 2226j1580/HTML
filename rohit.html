Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an
Indian international cricketer who currently captains
the India national cricket team across all formats.
He is a right-handed batsman. Considered one of
the best batsmen of his generation and one of the
greatest opening batters of all time, Sharma is
known for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities
and leadership skills.

IPL RECORDS

He made his IPL debut in 2008. Rohit Sharma has played 257 matches
to date in his IPL career and has scored 6,628 runs, with an average of
29.72. He has also slammed 2 centuries and 43 half-centuries, with his
highest IPL score being 109* runs. Rohit Sharma has hit 599 fours and
280 sixes in his IPL career.In the IPL Auction 2024, Mumbai franchise
acquired the services of Rohit Sharma for Rs 16.00 Cr. On May 2024,
he played his last IPL match against Lucknow the Wankhede Stadium,
Mumbai, India, scoring 68 runs off 38 balls.
